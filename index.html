Our Story

Since our humble beginnings, ALSEHE RESTAURANT has been built on a passion for authentic Middle Eastern flavors. What started as a family kitchen grew into a place where tradition and hospitality come together.

From the crunch of freshly made kibbeh to the warmth of shakriya and the variety of mezze, every dish tells a story of heritage, love, and community.

Today, we are proud to share these flavors with you, inviting you to experience the taste of the East in every bite.

Meet Our Chef

Chef Ahmad is the creative force behind our kitchen. With years of experience in Middle Eastern cuisine, he masterfully combines traditional recipes with modern techniques.

His philosophy is simple: fresh ingredients, authentic flavors, and a touch of passion in every dish.
